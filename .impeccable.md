## Design Context

### Users
Freelancers (developers, designers) and clients who need safe P2P payments for project work. They operate in Telegram-centric communities, often Russian-speaking but the UI is English. They're comfortable with crypto (TON) but want simplicity — not DeFi complexity. They use the platform in two contexts: quick actions in Telegram (Mini App, inline bot) and deeper browsing on web (market, offers, profiles).

### Brand Personality
**Fast, Bold, Crypto-native** — Move-fast energy with web3 roots. Not corporate, not playful. Technical confidence without being intimidating. The product speaks through its speed and AI capabilities, not marketing fluff.

### Emotional Goal
**Confidence & Safety** — "My money is secure, I can trust this." Despite the bold/fast personality, the core emotional contract is trust. Every interaction should reinforce that funds are protected by smart contracts, AI is watching for fairness, and the system is transparent.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Dark-only. Base `#0f0f1a`, surfaces `rgba(255,255,255,0.03-0.05)`
- **Accent:** TON blue `#0098EA` with gradient to `#00D1FF`. Used sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- **Style:** Glass morphism (backdrop-blur, translucent borders), subtle glows, clean lines
- **Font:** Inter (300-600). Tight tracking on headings, relaxed on body
- **Icons:** Solar set (Iconify) — linear style, consistent 16-24px
- **Radius:** Large (2xl-3xl for cards, full for pills/badges)
- **Motion:** Subtle float animations, smooth transitions. No bouncy or playful motion — confident and measured

### References
- **Linear.app** — Clean dark UI, information density without clutter, premium developer feel
- **Telegram native** — Chat-first patterns, bottom sheets in mobile, familiar interaction patterns
- **TON ecosystem (Tonkeeper)** — Crypto-native visual language, wallet patterns, transaction cards

### Anti-References
- Generic SaaS dashboards with white backgrounds
- Overly playful/cartoon crypto apps (like early DeFi)
- Dense financial terminals (Bloomberg-style)
- Cookie-cutter Tailwind templates with no personality

### Design Principles

1. **Trust Through Transparency** — Show the state of things clearly. Escrow status, AI reasoning, trust scores, contract addresses. Hidden complexity erodes trust.

2. **Mobile-First, Chat-Native** — Primary interaction is Telegram. Bottom sheets over page navigations. Compact cards over sprawling layouts. Thumb-friendly targets.

3. **AI as Utility, Not Spectacle** — AI features (spec generation, pricing, matching, arbitration) should feel like tools, not demos. Show results, not process. No "AI is thinking..." theatrics beyond brief loading states.

4. **Progressive Disclosure** — Guests see value (jobs, prices), auth unlocks depth (contacts, proposals, AI insights). Never block — blur and invite.

5. **TON-Native Identity** — Prices in TON by default. Wallet integration is natural, not bolted-on. The blue gradient is the brand — use it for primary actions only.

### Component Patterns

- **Glass Card:** `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-lg rounded-2xl border border-white/10`
- **Primary Button:** `ton-gradient px-6 py-3 rounded-xl text-sm font-medium text-white`
- **Ghost Button:** `bg-white/5 border border-white/10 rounded-xl hover:bg-white/10`
- **Badge:** `px-2 py-0.5 rounded-full text-[10px] font-medium` + color variant
- **Bottom Sheet (mobile):** Slides up, ~80vh, rounded-t-3xl, backdrop blur overlay
- **Skeleton:** `bg-white/5 rounded-xl animate-pulse`
